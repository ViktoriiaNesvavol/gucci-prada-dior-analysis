Fashion Project: Wikidata & LLMs

Our Fashion Project

Our fashion project delves into Gucci, Prada, and Dior, utilizing the rich data available in the Wikidata Knowledge Graph. As a comprehensive and well-structured knowledge base, Wikidata was invaluable for uncovering facts about fashion, especially within the luxury segment. To overcome any data deficiencies, we implemented Large Language Models (LLMs) such as ChatGPT and Gemini to generate supplementary information. Our team then manually verified and refined these generated insights against the Wikidata ontology to maintain accuracy and coherence.

Brands Explored

We focused on three iconic fashion houses: **Gucci**, **Prada**, and **Dior**. Each offers a unique perspective on luxury fashion, providing a rich dataset for our analysis.

Wikidata Integration

Leveraging the comprehensive **Wikidata Knowledge Graph** allowed us to extract structured information about these brands, their history, collections, and associated figures.

LLM Enhancement

To address data gaps, we employed **Large Language Models (LLMs)** like ChatGPT and Gemini. Their generative capabilities helped fill in missing details, which were then manually verified for accuracy.
